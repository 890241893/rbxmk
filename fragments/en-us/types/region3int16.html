An axis-aligned rectangular cuboid with 16-bit integer precision.

The Region3 type represents an axis-aligned three-dimensional rectangular cuboid with a lower and upper boundary, with each component having 16-bit integer precision.

Returns Region3 composed by two Vector3s.

The new constructor returns a Region3 composed by two Vector3int16 values, where min is the lower bound of the region, and max is the upper bound.

The upper bounds of the region.

The Max field returns the upper bounds of the region.

The lower bounds of the region.

The Min field returns the lower bounds of the region.

Returns whether two Region3int16 values are equal.

The equal operator returns true if both operands are Region3int16, and each corresponding component is equal.
